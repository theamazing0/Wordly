Wordly

By Samvid Konchada

7-8 Project Programming

59%

of web content is in English

Only 18%

of the world can speak English

Wordly is a browser extention to help English learners while they browse the web.

The user can select articles from the internet and read them with Wordly.

Depending on the user's familiarity of English, Wordly can translate sentences they won't understand in English to their native language.

It also helps users understand new words by putting information a click away.
